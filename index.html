FileForge - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

FileForge does not collect, transmit, or store any of your files or personal data.

What data we collect

FileForge collects no personal data. All PDF and image processing happens entirely inside your browser using client-side JavaScript. Your files are never uploaded to any server.

License key validation

When you activate a Pro license, the extension sends your license key to the Gumroad API (api.gumroad.com) solely to verify its validity. No file content or personal information is included. The key is stored locally using chrome.storage.local.

Third-party services

FileForge Pro licenses are sold via Gumroad. Gumroad's own privacy policy applies to purchases made on their platform.
